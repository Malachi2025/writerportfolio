This is a space for writers at every stage. We read every submission with care and respond to all within six weeks. We are particularly drawn to work that takes risks — with form, with silence, with the unsayable.

Guidelines

  • Short stories & prose: up to 5,000 words
  • Poetry: up to three pieces
  • Essays: up to 4,000 words
  • Novel excerpts: up to 5,000 words + synopsis
  • Simultaneous submissions accepted
  • No previously published work
  • All work read anonymously
  • Response within six weeks
