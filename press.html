Short Biography

Peter Hace is an award-winning author of literary fiction and the founder of Spark. Write. Inspire. His work has been translated into fourteen languages and longlisted for the Booker Prize.

He is a graduate of the Iowa Writers’ Workshop and has taught at Columbia University and the Bread Loaf Writers’ Conference. He lives between New York and County Clare, Ireland.
