Short Biography

Peter Hace is an award-winning author of literary fiction and founder of Spark. Write. Inspire. His work has been translated into fourteen languages and longlisted for the Booker Prize.

A graduate of the Iowa Writers’ Workshop, he has taught at Columbia University and Bread Loaf. He lives between New York and County Clare, Ireland.
